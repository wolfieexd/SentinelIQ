# SentinelIQ: Pitch Script (2-3 Minutes)

**[Introduction - On Home Screen]**
"Hi everyone, we are the team behind **SentinelIQ**. 
Security analysts today are drowning in alerts. They don't need another chatbot. They need an autonomous investigation platform that acts like a senior incident responder. 
Let me show you how SentinelIQ handles a critical Insider Threat."

**[Click "Insider Threat"]**

**[During Workspace Animation (0-15s)]**
"Behind the scenes, a swarm of specialized AI agents is currently ripping through thousands of raw logs. 
Look at the **Attack Story** on the left. The agents have reconstructed the timeline: A badge was swiped at 3 AM, followed by a massive SharePoint download and a USB connection.
But here is where we stand out. Notice the **Compliance Guardrail** panel lighting up. Security AI is dangerous if it's unconstrained. Our dedicated guardrail agent just intercepted a massive PII exfiltration attempt in real-time."

**[During Decision Center Generation (15-20s)]**
"Now the agents are generating a response plan. But LLMs hallucinate. 
In a SOC, you can't rely on guesses. So we built **Foundry IQ**."

**[Click the [Why?] button]**
"When the agent recommends isolating the endpoint, it proves *why*. SentinelIQ uses a semantic RAG pipeline powered by FAISS to retrieve our exact Enterprise Security Policies and MITRE frameworks. The action is grounded in our actual corporate playbooks."

**[War Room Auto-Opens]**
"Finally, the investigation completes, and SentinelIQ generates the **War Room**. 
A completely correlated evidence graph, a full timeline, and a transparent log of every AI thought process. 
SentinelIQ doesn't just find the threat—it investigates it, grounds the response in policy, and packages it for the executive team. 
Thank you."
